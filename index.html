About Us

Hot Beans Web was started in 2015 by Arthur Alexander, growing from him being the sole web developer to the team of 4 we have today. His prior years of experience in the web development industry have helped the company grow to where we are today.

We believe in form & function working in synergy and that true craftsmanship is when the two come together, not one in front of the other.

Designing something without an understanding of how it works is to fail from the outset. When we ask ourselves 'Does it look good?', we also think 'Will it work well?'.

We go above and beyond for our clients. Knowing that we must not only produce better work than our peers, but also push the boundaries of creativity and innovation.

Our team is passionate about web design with their mission to bring both design and technology together. We always try to go beyond our client's expectation.
